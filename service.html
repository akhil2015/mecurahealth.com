Our Services

Clinic

An establishment or hospital department where outpatients are given medical treatment or advice, especially of a specialist nature.

On-Demand Doctor

Available when you are and without the hassle of the waiting room. Connect in minutes with board-certified doctors and therapists over live video.

Home Consultant

A consultant is a professional who provides expert advice in a particular area

Physiotherapy

Physical therapy, mostly known as Physiotherapy, is a primary care specialty in western medicine that, by using mechanical force and movements.

Laboratory Service

A clinical laboratory is a laboratory where tests are usually done on clinical specimens in order to obtain information about the health of a patient as pertaining to the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease.

Pharmacy Service

Pharmacy is the science and technique of preparing and dispensing drugs. It is a health profession that links health sciences with chemical sciences and aims to ensure the safe and effective use of pharmaceutical drugs.

Ambulance Service

An ambulance is a vehicle for transportation of ill or injured people. Our ambulance works 24 hours a day and 7 days a week even at mid night. Just call on emergency number.

Nursing

Nursing is the protection, promotion, and optimization of health and abilities, prevention of illness and injury, facilitation of healing, alleviation of suffering through the diagnosis and treatment of human response, and advocacy in the care of individuals, families, groups, communities, and populations.

Emergengy Services

The public organizations that respond to and deal with emergencies when they occur, especially the ambulance service, the police, and the fire brigade. Call us on emergency number.
