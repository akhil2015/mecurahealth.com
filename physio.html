What is physiotherapy?

Physiotherapy is a recognized health care discipline that specializes in the assessment, treatment and maintenance of the human bodyâ€™s movement and function. Physiotherapists integrate the use of many therapeutic modalities with their expertise in exercise and movement rehabilitation to create the optimal plan to meet your therapeutic goals. Mecura eClinic focuses on providing physiotherapeutic interventions and exercise programs to people of all ages presenting with any type of orthopaedic condition, which is any condition related to bone, muscle and joint problems.

What does physiotherapy treatment entail?

Your rehabilitation will include one-on-one manual treatments by your therapist aimed at alleviating pain and increasing movement. Your therapist may also use various modalities to speed your recovery and decrease pain. Your therapist will also prescribe a specific exercise program to improve your strength, flexibility or function as needed. This program will be closely monitored and progressed by your therapist. Mecura eClinic, each program is customized to suit your individual needs with the goal of getting you back to normal activity independently and as soon as possible. Should you require other services, such as massage therapy or custom orthotics assessments to further aid in your recovery.
